Core principles

Built for enterprise.
Designed for architects.

K9-AIF brings the rigour of SOA, TOGAF, and OOA/OOD to the agentic AI layer.

⬡

ABB / SBB separation

Architecture Building Blocks define stable contracts. Solution Building Blocks extend them with domain-specific behavior — without touching the core.

⚙

Configuration-driven composition

Agents, orchestrators, squads, and routing strategies are wired via YAML — no hardcoded dependencies, no brittle imports.

🔒

Governance as a first-class concern

Policy enforcement, allow/deny lists, compliance controls, and audit trails are built into the inference layer — not bolted on afterward.

⇄

Provider-independent inference

The Model Router and LLMFactory abstract away provider specifics. Swap models or vendors without changing application logic.

◎

Session-aware routing

SQLite out of the box, PostgreSQL for enterprise. Routing decisions, model affinity, and session state are persisted and auditable.

↗

Extensible by design

Every ABB is a contract. Introduce new routing strategies, orchestration patterns, or storage backends as SBBs — the architecture stays stable.
